Use Case: AI-Guided SOAP Note with Minimal Typing

Scenario:
Dr. Ayesha has a 35-year-old male patient with tooth sensitivity. She wants a SOAP note fast, without typing it all herself.

1. Trigger with text expander

  • Typing /soapdent pastes her reusable prompt:

“You are a dental assistant. I’ll give gender, age, and main complaint.
Ask follow-up questions, refine diagnosis, suggest treatment, and create a SOAP note.”

2. Provide minimal details

“35-year-old male, sensitivity in upper right molar.”

3. AI guides the process

  • AI asks targeted history/exam questions.
  • She answers in short bullet points.

4. AI refines diagnosis

“Early occlusal caries with dentin hypersensitivity (#16).”

5. AI suggests treatment

  • Minimal cavity prep + composite restoration
  • Desensitizing agent if needed
  • Fluoride toothpaste & review in 6 months

6. AI outputs full SOAP note
Ready to paste directly into records — total time: under 3 minutes.

What Makes This Work

  1. Reusable base prompt
  2. Text expander shortcut
  3. AI that asks, not just answers
  4. Concise dentist input
  5. Quick review before saving

With this setup, AI becomes your real-time thinking partner — not just a typing assistant.
